This is me

I am a Web Design student at University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Clinical Psychology and passionate about Psychoanalysis and art. I hope that helped explain the combination of creative possibilities on this website.
I want to create a space that can combine my knowledge and what I enjoy the most. After many years of going to psychoanalytical therapy, it is something I want to be a part of for my current and future patients.
